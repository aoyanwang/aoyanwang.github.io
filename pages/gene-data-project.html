Gene Data Analysis Project

This is a self-initiated project focused on establishing an automated pipeline for the acquisition, processing, and refinement of large-scale datasets (Big Data). The core objectives are to gain a comprehensive understanding of the theoretical and technical principles underlying data collection, and to master the methodologies and logical frameworks essential for efficient data preparation and transformation. This exploration serves as a practical pathway to delve into the intricacies, mysteries, and fundamental workings of the electronic and computer science domains.

Project Details

  • Data sources: ClinVar, ClinGen (notes), potential DisGeNET/OMIM expansion
  • Processing: VCF parsing, variant filtering, annotation
  • Outputs: Variant tables, plots, summary reports
