Athletics & Endurance

I have loved water since I was two years old. Playing in swimming pools gave me endless joy and gradually built both skill and confidence. In kindergarten, I was selected by Coach Wu Ying, a championship coach from Chen Jinglun Youth Swimming Team, and began six years of semi-professional training. Although I chose not to pursue competitive swimming as a career, the discipline and endurance I developed have shaped my approach to learning and life.

In Australia, I swam in the ocean almost every day—one session for swimming, another for surfing. I received multiple awards at school swimming carnivals and was invited to the Illawarra Regional Championships, though I fell ill just before the competition. After returning to China, I joined the school basketball and orienteering teams, earning medals in track and field events. Now in Canada, I play badminton nearly every day and continue swimming weekly. Sports have brought me health, friendships, and the joy of teamwork—values that carry into all areas of my life.
